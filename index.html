Frequently Asked Questions

What should I carry?

Comfortable shoes, water (1–2L), light snacks, hat, and a light jacket for wind.

Are the hikes beginner friendly?

Yes. We group hikers by pace and offer beginner options on most trails.

What time do hikes start?

Most hikes start between 6:30–8:00 AM; sunrise options start earlier.

How do I confirm my spot?

Message us on WhatsApp, pick a date, then pay via M-Pesa to secure your slot.
