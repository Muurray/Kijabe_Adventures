♻ Our Flagship Conservation Project

Sustainable Briquette Production

We convert biomass waste into eco-friendly charcoal briquettes, reducing deforestation while creating employment opportunities.

With proper machinery and production facilities, this project can become a self-sustaining community enterprise.

Why Partnership Matters

Kijabe Adventures is more than just a hiking group — we are a growing movement dedicated to:

  • ✔ Promoting safe and responsible hiking in Kenya
  • ✔ Supporting environmental conservation through tree planting and trail clean-ups
  • ✔ Training local guides and youth to create sustainable livelihoods
  • ✔ Restoring and maintaining hiking trails to protect local ecosystems
  • ✔ Building a community that values nature, wellness, and outdoor adventure

Every partnership helps us keep hikers safe, protect natural areas, and empower communities.
