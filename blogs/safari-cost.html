How Expensive Is a Safari in Kenya?

Published 2026 • By Kijabe Adventures Team

Kenya offers safari options for every budget—from backpacker-friendly to ultra-luxury. Prices depend on the park, season, accommodation type, and how long you stay.

Typical Safari Costs

  • Budget Safari: $150–$250 per day
  • Mid-range Safari: $300–$600 per day
  • Luxury Safari: $800–$2,000+ per day

What Affects Safari Prices?

1. National Park Fees — e.g., Maasai Mara now charges $100–$200 per day depending on season.

2. Accommodation — campsites, lodges, luxury camps.

3. Transport — safari jeep, fuel, guide.

4. Group Size — larger groups = cheaper per person.

Kenya has a safari for every budget as long as you plan properly.
