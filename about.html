About Kijabe Adventures

Kijabe Adventures is a locally-led hiking and tourism team offering outdoor experiences across Kenya — not just in Kijabe. Our guided trips cover regions such as Mt. Longonot, Ngong Hills, Mt. Suswa, Aberdares, Naivasha, and the Rift Valley Escarpments.

While Kijabe Hills and Kijabe Forest remain our home base, our passion extends across the country. We work closely with local communities, scouts, and environmental partners to keep trails safe, clean, and sustainable.
