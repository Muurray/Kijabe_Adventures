About Kijabe Adventures

Local guides promoting safe, sustainable hiking across Kenya’s most scenic landscapes.

Who We Are

Kijabe Adventures is a locally-led hiking and outdoor experience team based around Kijabe, Kijabe Hills, and Kijabe Forest — some of Kenya’s most beautiful and underrated hiking landscapes. Our mission is to make hiking accessible, safe, and enjoyable for everyone, from first-time hikers to seasoned explorers.

We understand the trails because we live here. We work closely with local communities, forest rangers, and environmental partners to keep trails open, clean, and sustainable for future generations.

Our Values

  • Safety First – All guides are trained in first aid, navigation, and responsible guiding.
  • Local Knowledge – We support community-based tourism that directly benefits local families.
  • Conservation – We promote trail cleanups, forest protection, and low-impact hiking.
  • Inclusivity – We welcome hikers of all levels, ages, and abilities.

Our Mission

Our goal is to promote safe hiking, environmental education, and sustainable tourism across Kenya. When you hike with us, you support:

Safety Training

Guides trained in wilderness first aid and emergency response.

Trail Cleanups

Organized monthly trash cleanups within Kijabe Forest & other trails.

Community Support

Partnerships with local youth groups and community rangers.
