Protecting Nature

Our community believes in leaving every trail better than we found it. We organize regular clean-up hikes where volunteers help remove waste from forests, hills, and trails across Kenya.

  • Trail clean-ups
  • Tree planting activities
  • Environmental awareness hikes

At Kijabe Adventures, we believe that exploring nature also means protecting it. Our hiking community actively participates in conservation activities that help preserve Kenya’s forests, trails, and natural landscapes.

🌍 Clean-Up Hikes

We organize regular clean-up hikes where volunteers help remove waste from trails, forests, and hills. These events keep our hiking routes beautiful while raising awareness about environmental conservation.

🌳 Tree Planting Partnerships

We are also looking for individuals, organizations, schools, and community groups interested in organizing tree planting events in Kijabe Forest and other forests across Kenya.

Tree planting activities can be organized to mark special occasions or important environmental events.

  • 🌿 Corporate social responsibility (CSR) events
  • 🎓 School environmental programs
  • 🎉 Birthdays and special celebrations
  • 🌍 World Environment Day activities
  • 🏢 Company team-building events
