Guided Private Hiking Tours Across Kenya

Kijabe Adventures organizes private group hikes across Kenya for corporate teams, schools, churches, families, friends and travel groups seeking memorable outdoor experiences.

Our most popular hiking destinations include Kijabe Hills (Kijabe River Walk Trails, Kiharu Waterfalls, and Kijabe Railway Trail), as well as Ngong Hills, Mt Longonot and Karura Forest.

Whether you want a beginner-friendly nature walk, a scenic waterfall adventure, or a challenging mountain hike, we provide experienced local guides, organized logistics and customized hiking packages.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do private group hikes cost in Kenya?

Private hiking costs depend on destination, group size, residency and optional services such as parking and transport. Use our instant price calculator above to get your exact quote.

Do you organize corporate team building hikes?

Yes. We organize corporate hiking experiences for companies, organizations and professional teams across Kenya. Contact us on WhatsApp to discuss your group's needs.

Are the hikes beginner friendly?

Yes. We offer beginner-friendly hikes, moderate hikes and advanced adventure hikes tailored to your group's fitness level.

How far in advance should I book a private group hike?

We recommend booking at least 3–7 days in advance to confirm guide availability and logistics. For larger groups (20+), 2 weeks notice is ideal.
